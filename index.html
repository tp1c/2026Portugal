💡 Lisbon Walkability Pro-Tips

Lisbon is built on extremely steep hills. Walkways are paved in traditional calçada portuguesa (cobblestones) which are highly slick when worn down or damp. High-traction rubber soles are mandatory. Avoid unnecessary uphill climbs by utilizing vertical transit shortcuts (metro station escalators, public lifts, and vintage Tram 25).

🏨 Logistics & Walkability: Viking Transfer included. Corinthia Hotel (Avenida Columbano Bordalo Pinheiro 105).

Quick run for local Portuguese wines, fresh bakery items, and snacks. Avoid overpriced hotel shops—this is where the local neighborhood shops.

🚶 Logistics & Walkability: 5-10 min flat walk from the hotel.
Transit to Belém 3:30 PM

Your free time begins at 3:00 PM. Taking an Uber from the hotel to Belém is highly recommended; it is faster, more comfortable, and often more economical than public transit for two people.

🚇 Logistics & Walkability: Order an Uber directly from The Corinthia Lisbon to the Torre de Belém (15-20 min). Or take Metro Blue Line to Baixa-Chiado, switch to Green to Cais do Sodré, and catch train to Belém.
The Age of Discovery Waterfront 4:00 PM

Admire the 16th-century Torre de Belém from the exterior boardwalks to avoid the grueling 90-minute wait for the interior stairs. Stroll down the path to the massive Monument to the Discoveries (Padrão dos Descobrimentos).

🚶 Logistics & Walkability: 100% Flat. Walk along a paved pedestrian path that skirts the edge of the Tagus River.
Pastéis de Belém 5:30 PM

Bypass the massive line outside on the sidewalk—that is only for takeout. Walk straight inside to the historic inner rooms to sit down and enjoy original warm custard tarts (pastéis de nata).

🚶 Logistics & Walkability: Short flat walk from the waterfront to the main road.
Dinner at LX Factory (Alcântara) 6:30 PM

Avoid the overpriced restaurants in Belém. Head to LX Factory—an abandoned textile factory transformed into a trendy, creative village filled with local artists, shops, and highly-rated restaurants.

🚶 Logistics & Walkability: 20-minute flat walk from Belém along the Tagus River, or a quick 5-minute Uber ride.
Return to Hotel 8:30 PM

Return to the Corinthia Lisbon hotel. Refresh and relax after your first day in Portugal.

🚇 Logistics & Walkability: Grab a quick Uber from LX Factory back to The Corinthia, or take an Uber to the city center for around €4 to catch the Metro back to Sete Rios.
